LIGHTING RAJAGOPALAPURAM

The start of an illuminating journey.

A small village under Ladduvadi Panchayath in Talavasal Taluk of Salem district, Tamil Nadu. Located 77 Km towards east from district headquarters Salem, this place is 270 Km from State capital Chennai.

Rajagopalapuram is surrounded by Chinnasalem taluk towards east, Attur & Gangavalli taluk towards west and Veppanthattai taluk towards south. With the nearby cities like Thammampatti, Perambalur, Tittakudi and Thuraiyur, Rajagopalapuram is siuated in the border of Salem and Villapuram districts.

Chinnasalem being situated towards east to Rajgopalapuram, public and private transport is accessible here.
